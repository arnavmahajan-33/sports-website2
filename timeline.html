A journey through the wonderful career of Roger Federer.

  • November 14th, 2005 35-match win streak 2005. This is tied for eighth longest all-surface streak of the Open Era.
  • February 27th, 2006 56-match hard court win streak 2005–2006. This is the longest hard court streak of the Open Era.
  • August 14th, 2006 49-match win streak in the United States 2004–2006. This is the longest streak on American soil of the Open Era.
  • March 5th, 2007 41-match win streak 2006–2007. This is the seventh longest all-surface streak of the Open Era.
  • June 23rd, 2008 65-match grass court win streak 2003–2008. This is the longest grass streak of the Open Era.
